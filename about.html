About Us

Restaurant is a Jalandhar based collective of restaurants founded by Md Tabish Farhan and Vishant. First founded in 2023 with a single venue, it has now grown to several, all based in Punjab’s Fortitude Valley, with plans for many more. The existing venues are some of Jalandhar's most critically acclaimed establishments; that individually embody the values and the culinary culture of Punjab, through their unique individual personalities. They include award-winning restaurants, bars and a bakery.
